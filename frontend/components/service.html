Our Services

Document Verification

Idwall verifies the authenticity of documents such as RG (Identity Card), CPF (Individual Taxpayer Identification Number), CNH (Driver's License), and proof of residence, assisting companies in ensuring they are dealing with legitimate information and real identities.

Risk Analysis

Through risk analysis, Idwall assesses the reliability of information provided by individuals, aiding in real-time decision-making, such as the approval of financial transactions.

Digital Onboarding

Idwall offers digital onboarding solutions for businesses, allowing them to expedite customer integration by securely and swiftly verifying identity information.

Fraud Detection

Idwall employs advanced techniques to identify potential fraud, such as verifying inconsistent or suspicious information, aiding in protection against fraudulent activities.

Email and Phone Number Validation

The company validates the legitimacy of email addresses and phone numbers, helping reduce the occurrence of false or non-existent information in customer databases.

Background Check

Idwall conducts background checks, including criminal history and sanctions list queries, to ensure companies have complete and accurate information about their customers.

Compliance

The company assists with compliance with regulations and laws by providing verification tools that help businesses meet legal and regulatory requirements.

Anti-Money Laundering (AML)

Idwall helps businesses identify suspicious activities that may indicate money laundering, thereby ensuring the integrity of financial transactions.
